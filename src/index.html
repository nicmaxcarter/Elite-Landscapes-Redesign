Elite Landscapes was established full time in 2013 by Grayson Smith after graduating from Lowndes High. He took his passion for being outside and working hard and made it into a career. From the humble beginnings of a young dream, the company has now grown into much more including: Landscape installation, Landscape Lighting, turf and ornamental care, paver installation, full service lawn maintenance, irrigation, and drainage. A true vision for creating and maintaining beautiful landscapes paired with a high priority on customer service is what sets Elite Landscapes apart from the rest.
